Job Hunter Toolkit

Works with any AI. Works with no AI.

Your job search.
Your data.
Your AI.

A structured methodology for running a job search — built on plain Markdown files you own. No account, no credits, no data sent anywhere.

How it works

1
Build your career narrative

A guided interview surfaces your real stories, metrics, and voice. The foundation every application draws from.

2
Analyze job postings

Paste a posting, get a pre-filled analysis template: what they actually need, where you match, apply/skip/stretch verdict.

3
Track applications

Log every application. The patterns section tells you where the funnel leaks and what to fix.

4
Bring your AI (optional)

Drop this folder into Claude, ChatGPT, or Gemini and say "read the docs/ folder." The AI absorbs the instructions and runs the session. Everything exports as Markdown your AI can read and write.
