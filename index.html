Job Hunter Assistant

Works with any AI. Works with no AI.

Your job search.
Your data.
Your AI.

A structured methodology for running a job search — built on plain Markdown files you own. No account, no credits, no data sent anywhere.

How it works

1
Build your career narrative

A guided interview surfaces your real stories, metrics, and voice. The foundation every application draws from.

2
Analyze job postings

Paste a posting, get a pre-filled analysis template: what they actually need, where you match, apply/skip/stretch verdict.

3
Track applications

Log every application. The patterns section tells you where the funnel leaks and what to fix.

4
Bring your AI (optional)

Drop this folder into Claude, ChatGPT, or Gemini and say "read the docs/ folder." The AI absorbs the instructions and runs the session. Everything exports as Markdown your AI can read and write.

Career Narrative

Answer the questions. Export your narrative as a Markdown file when done — save it to private/career-narrative.md in the assistant folder.

Fresh Jobs

Pulled straight from company job boards (Greenhouse, Lever, Ashby), filtered and scored against your watchlist. Everything lands in private/jobs/ as Markdown.

The scraper needs the local server.

From the assistant folder, run python3 serve.py and open the address it prints. The server runs only on your machine — nothing leaves it.

This is private/watchlist.md — companies to watch, title filters, boost keywords, locations. Plain Markdown; your AI can edit it too.

JD Analysis

Paste a job posting. Export a pre-filled analysis template — save it to private/jd-[company]-[role].md and work through it with or without your AI.
